Frequently asked questions

Where is my data stored?

Your stack, doses, goals, and reports stay on your device. There is no account, and we have no servers that hold this data. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do purchases work?

Auditly offers an Audit Pass (7-day full-report access) and a Year Pass (365-day access). Both are one-time purchases billed through the App Store — nothing auto-renews, and access simply ends when a pass expires.

Is Auditly medical advice?

No. Auditly is an educational decision-support tool. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any condition, and it is not a substitute for professional care. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional or pharmacist about your individual situation.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete all of your data at any time from Settings inside the app.
