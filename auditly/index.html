Auditly

See your supplement stack clearly.

Auditly reviews the supplements you take and shows you where they may overlap, where a dose may be worth a closer look, when to take each one, where you may be overspending, and what may be worth discussing with a clinician — all based on the information you enter, in cautious, plain language.

Everything you take, in one place

Add a product by hand, or scan its Supplement Facts label and confirm the details. Auditly reads each ingredient and dose so your whole stack is in one view.

A clear, cautious read on your stack

Run an audit and Auditly explains what it finds in plain, non-medical language:

  • Ingredients that overlap across more than one product
  • Doses that may be worth a closer look
  • Where overlap may be costing you money
  • What may be worth raising with a clinician

Know when to take each one

Auditly suggests an order and time of day for your stack, based on how things absorb and when they're conventionally taken — informational, from what you entered.

See what changed

Edit your stack and re-run an audit to see exactly what moved since last time — new overlaps, changed verdicts, and how your potential savings shifted.

Private by design

Your stack stays on your device. There is no account, we have no servers that hold it, and we never sell your data. Read the Privacy Policy.
