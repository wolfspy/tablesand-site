Your stack

Everything you take, in one place.

Add a product by hand, or scan its Supplement Facts label and confirm the details. Auditly reads each ingredient and dose so your whole stack lives in one clean view.

The audit

A clear, cautious read on your stack.

Run an audit and Auditly gives every product a plain verdict - Keep, Cut, Replace, or Ask a clinician - and explains why in non-medical language:

  • ✓Ingredients that overlap across more than one product
  • ✓Doses that may be worth a closer look
  • ✓Where overlap may be costing you money
  • ✓What may be worth raising with a clinician
Timing

Know when to take each one.

Auditly suggests an order and time of day for your stack - morning, with dinner, before sleep - based on how things absorb and when they're conventionally taken. Informational, built from what you entered.

Over time

See exactly what changed.

Edit your stack and re-run an audit to see what moved since last time - new overlaps, changed verdicts, and how your potential monthly savings shifted.

More room on iPad

On iPad, Auditly opens into a two-column view - browse by goal, scan safety watch-outs, and explore early-evidence leads tied to what you already take. Same private app, more space to think.
