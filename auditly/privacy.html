Privacy Policy

Tablesand LLC · Colorado, USA

We treat your supplement and health-context data as sensitive, and we built Auditly so that it stays on your device.

Stays on your device — we never receive it

  • Your stack, doses, costs, goals, reports, and any health-context answers (such as medication use, pregnancy, or health conditions) are stored only on this device, protected by your device's standard data protection (and any backups you choose to make). There is no account, we have no servers that hold this data, and we cannot see it.

What leaves your device, and to whom

  • Automatic label reading: when you scan a label, your device reads the text from it, and that label text is sent to our cloud function and to Google's Gemini AI service to structure it into fields you then confirm. We send label text only — never your photos, your goals, or your health-context answers. Google processes this text under terms that do not permit using it to train its models.
  • Crash and diagnostic data: collected through Google Firebase Crashlytics to fix stability problems. It is kept separate from your supplement and health inputs.
  • Purchases: handled by Apple through the App Store. We receive only your access entitlement — never your name, card, or payment details.
  • App-integrity checks: an Apple App Attest token confirms the app is genuine to prevent abuse. It contains no personal data.

Who we share data with

  • Google (Firebase / Gemini) as our service provider, and Apple for purchases. We do not share your data with anyone else. See Google's and Apple's privacy terms for how they handle data we send them.

What we never do

  • We never sell your personal or health data.
  • We do not run ads, use ad-based monetization, or use any analytics SDK.
  • We do not use your health inputs for marketing.

Your controls

  • Delete all of your data at any time from Settings. Deletion removes your stack and reports from this device. It cannot reach diagnostic logs already sent to our crash-reporting provider, or label text already processed by the AI service.

Your U.S. privacy rights

  • Depending on your state (for example California, Virginia, Colorado, or Connecticut), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or limit the use of your personal information, and to not be discriminated against for exercising them. Because we store nothing on our servers, do not sell your data, and run no targeted advertising, deleting your data in the app satisfies most requests. For anything else, contact us at privacy@tablesand.com.

Consumer health data (including Washington and Nevada residents)

  • Supplement information can be treated as consumer health data under some state laws. We do not sell it, we do not use it for advertising, and the stack and health-context answers you enter never leave your device — so we do not "collect" them under those laws. The only health-related information that leaves your device is the label text you scan, processed only as necessary to give you that scan, not retained by us, and never used for any other purpose. Washington and Nevada residents may have additional rights regarding consumer health data; contact privacy@tablesand.com to exercise them.

Children

  • Auditly is not directed to anyone under 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Contact

  • Questions about privacy: privacy@tablesand.com.

Auditly is currently offered only in the United States. This policy covers the Auditly app; this website itself collects nothing beyond the standard server logs our hosting provider keeps to deliver the pages you request.
